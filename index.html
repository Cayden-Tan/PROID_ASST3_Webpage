ACEhub is transforming the space under the Anak Bukit Flyover into a well-being hub with events, activities and art.

*Project is in development

This space has been redesigned to serve the community better, offering a variety of facilities, activities and events that promote well-being and community engagement.

Whether you're looking for a place to relax, exercise, or participate in community events, ACEhub provides the perfect setting. Explore the different sections of our website to find out more about the events and activities we offer.

Features

Seating Areas

The space will include a thoughtfully designed seating area with benches and tables, providing a versatile and inviting environment for the community. This feature will allow people to have a seat and relax, or do their work comfortably. Benches and tables will be made from weather-resistant materials like treated wood, metal, or recycled plastic, ensuring logevity and minimal maintenance.

Mental-Wellness Corner

The Mental-Wellness Corner will have a room to host mental wellness events and activities. The room will be designed to be condusive and mindfulness to allow people to express any feelings or emotions.

To make use of more sustainable materials, the room will be constructed will recycled shipping containers instead of raw materails like concrete and bricks that are not as sustainable.

Stage

There will be a stage constructed in the space. This will facilitate any of the events, activities or any performances for the community in the area. The stage can also be stowed away when not in use, to ensure that it does not cause any obstructions.
