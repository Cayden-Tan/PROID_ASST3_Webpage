ACE HUB is transforming the space under the Anak Bukit Flyover into a well-being hub with events, activities and art.

*Project is in development

This space has been redesigned to serve the community better, offering a variety of facilities, activities and events that promote well-being and community engagement.

Whether you're looking for a place to relax, exercise, or participate in community events, ACEhub provides the perfect setting. Explore the different sections of our website to find out more about the events and activities we offer.

Features

Seating Areas

The space will include a thoughtfully designed seating area with benches and tables, providing a versatile and inviting environment for the community. This feature will allow people to have a seat and relax, or do their work comfortably. Benches and tables will be made from weather-resistant materials like treated wood, metal, or recycled plastic, ensuring logevity and minimal maintenance.

Art Corner

Previous Next

The Arts Corner will also feature a art exhibit to showcase any artworks from partnering artists or any artworks that may be created in the space by the community during events and activities. The fixtures used for the art exhibit will also be made from recycled wooden pallets to promote sustainability and environmental friendliness.

There will also be a extended area for the Art Corner with benches and tables. This space will allow arts-based events and actvities to be organised to allow members of the community to further involve themselves in art.

This space can help to inculcate a sense of art appreciation among the community and to further improve the local art scene in Singapore

Mental-Wellness Corner

Previous Next

The Mental-Wellness Corner will have a room and tentages to host mental wellness events and activities. The room will be designed to be condusive and mindfulness to allow people to express any feelings or emotions. To make use of more sustainable materials, the room will be constructed will recycled shipping containers instead of raw materails like concrete and bricks to promote sustainability and reduce any potentail negative impacts to the environment.

The mental wellness corner will also have artificial grass flooring to allow the space to feel more comfortable and inviting. The inclusive of some simple on the floor board games will allow for people in the community to have fun to take their min.

The space will also feature a community notice board, that can display any posters for upcoming activities in the spaces as well as to promote tips improve personal mental wellness.

Stage

There will be a stage constructed in the space. This will facilitate any of the events, activities or any performances for the community in the area. The stage can also be stowed away when not in use, to ensure that it does not cause any obstructions.

There will also be stackable chairs that can be used for seating for stage events. The chairs can then be stowed under the stage when not in use
